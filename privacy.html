Privacy Policy for Atomic Guru

Last Updated: October 10, 2025

Thank you for using Atomic Guru ("the App"), developed by Sandeep Biswal G. Your privacy is of the utmost importance to us. This Privacy Policy is designed to be simple and clear: **Atomic Guru is a fully offline application and does not collect, store, or transmit any of your personal data.**

1. Data Collection and Usage

We collect absolutely no personal information. Because the App is designed to work completely offline, it has no need to collect data such as your name, email address, location, device identifiers, or usage analytics. All the data required for the app to function (the periodic table information) is included within the app itself.

2. App Permissions

Atomic Guru requires the following permission:

  • Internet: This permission is used **solely** for two optional, user-initiated features:
    1. Opening web links to Wikipedia or other external sites when you choose to.
    2. Loading the optional promotional image at the bottom of the detail screen. This is a one-way data request and does not transmit any information about you or your device.

No other permissions that could access personal data (like contacts, storage, or location) are requested.

3. Local Storage (Preferences)

The App uses your device's local storage (SharedPreferences) for the sole purpose of saving your settings. This includes:

  • Your chosen language (English or Odia).
  • Your list of favorite elements.
  • A flag to remember if you have completed the first-launch language selection.

This data is stored **only on your device**. It is never sent to us or any third party. If you uninstall the App, this data is deleted.

4. Third-Party Services

Atomic Guru **does not** integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising networks, or data collection services.

5. Children's Privacy

We do not knowingly collect any personally identifiable information from anyone. The App is safe for all ages as it does not collect data.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes. Changes to this Privacy Policy are effective when they are posted on this page.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, you can contact us:
